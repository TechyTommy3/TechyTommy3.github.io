How do I watch Tommy Weather on my TV?

This depends on the platform. But for most platforms, you can use your favorite IPTV server (Jellyfin and NextPVR are 2 popular ones) and connect your TV to it. Then you can watch and record it on your TV!

Where does the information come from?

(Simple version) All information comes from the National Weather Service.

(Advanced version) The data gets transmitted over XMPP on the NOAA Weather Wire network. Additional information comes from api.weather.gov.
